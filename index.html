OUR STORY

Opened in 2019 in Uptown Dallas, CutiePies Pizza & Bar is the culmination of years of thought and research by a handful of pizza enthusiasts on a quest to create the perfect pie. We all agree that pizza is life, so we traveled all around and tasted every combination of dough, crust, toppings, and type of oven imaginable. We think we came up with something kinda special and have the perfect casual environment to showcase our creation. But hey, it’s just pizza (and other good stuff), so relax, kick back, have a slice of the good life, and let us show you our take on your favorite dish.
